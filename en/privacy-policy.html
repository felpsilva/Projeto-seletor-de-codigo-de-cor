Introduction

The Online Color Selector (“we”, “our”, or “site”) values your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information, in compliance with **Law No. 13,709/2018 (LGPD)** and other applicable data protection regulations.

1. Information We Collect

Our site is designed to be used without the need for registration. Thus, we do not collect sensitive personal data or direct identification information, except in the cases described below:

a) Automatic collection (cookies and analytics)

We automatically collect some non-identifiable information, such as:

  • IP address (anonymized);
  • Browser type and version;
  • Pages accessed and visit time;
  • Traffic source (search, social media, etc.);
  • Usage data via **Google Analytics**.

This data is used exclusively for performance analysis and improving the user experience.

b) Optional interactions

If you contact us (via email or social media), we may store information such as your name and email address only to respond to the request. No data is shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Use of Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Analyze usage and behavior metrics on the site;
  • Improve tool performance, accessibility, and usability;
  • Correct errors and enhance features;
  • Ensure application security and integrity.

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We adopt appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, destruction, or alteration. The site is hosted on secure servers and follows good practices for **encryption and access control**.

4. Information Retention

Automatically collected data is stored only for the time necessary for performance analysis — generally between **14 and 26 months**, as per Google Analytics settings. After this period, they are **permanently deleted or anonymized**.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The site uses cookies to:

  • Remember language and theme preferences (dark or light mode);
  • Collect anonymous usage statistics.

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Disabling them may affect some functionalities, but the site will remain accessible.

The site may contain links to external pages, such as **GitHub** and **LinkedIn**. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We recommend reading the privacy policies of each accessed service.
